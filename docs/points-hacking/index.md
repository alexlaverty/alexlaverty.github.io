# Points hacking

This section covers earning and spending Australian loyalty points:
supermarket programs (Flybuys, Everyday Rewards), the two big frequent
flyer schemes (Qantas and Velocity), and the gift card strategies that
turn an ordinary grocery shop into flight rewards.
