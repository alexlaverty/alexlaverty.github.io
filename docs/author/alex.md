# Alex Laverty

I'm Alex Laverty. I'm a big data engineer based in Sydney, Australia, with
an interest in technology, photography, fitness, and building things that
are useful.

Outside of work, I like exploring ideas around self-reliance, sustainable
living, artificial intelligence, personal health, and ways technology can
help us better understand our lives. I enjoy experimenting with side
projects, automating everyday tasks, documenting places and experiences,
and learning how things work.

This site is a collection of those interests — a mix of notes, projects,
photos, experiments, and things I find interesting along the way.
