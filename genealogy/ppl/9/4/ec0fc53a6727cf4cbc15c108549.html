The Life Summary of Peter
When Peter McGree was born on 22 April 1876, in Tasmania, Australia, his father, James McGree, was 30 and his mother, Bridget Sullivan, was 23. He married Ethel Stella Cook in 1912, in Bowraville, New South Wales, Australia. They were the parents of at least 3 sons and 3 daughters. He died on 24 September 1949, in Taylors Arm, New South Wales, Australia, at the age of 73, and was buried in Macksville Cemetery, Macksville, New South Wales, Australia.

"Mr. Peter McGree has been suffering for some weeks from injuries to the left limbs received when an aerial pole fell upon him during the process of its erection."
