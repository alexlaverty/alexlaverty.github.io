Creating a day of eating that includes the highest and best sources for essential vitamins, minerals, and other super healthy compounds requires a well-rounded approach that covers both macronutrients (proteins, fats, and carbohydrates) and micronutrients (vitamins and minerals). Here's an optimal plan:

### Breakfast:
#### **Smoothie Bowl:**
- **Base**: **Kefir** (200 ml) – rich in **probiotics** and a good source of **calcium** and **vitamin D**.
- **Leafy Greens**: **Spinach** (1 cup) – loaded with **vitamin K, magnesium, and folate**.
- **Berries**: **Blueberries** (1/2 cup) – high in **vitamin C** and **antioxidants**.
- **Nuts/Seeds**: **Flaxseeds** (1 tbsp) – excellent source of **omega-3 fatty acids** and **fiber**.
- **Fruits**: **Papaya** (1/2 cup) – provides **vitamin A, vitamin C, and fiber**.
- **Protein**: **Whey protein isolate** (20g) – for high-quality protein and **B vitamins**.
- **Fat**: **Avocado** (1/2) – packed with **monounsaturated fats, potassium**, and **vitamin E**.

### Mid-Morning Snack:
#### **Almonds & Brazil Nuts**:
- **Almonds** (1 oz) – high in **vitamin E, magnesium**, and **healthy fats**.
- **Brazil nuts** (2 nuts) – a potent source of **selenium**, which supports thyroid function and immunity.

### Lunch:
#### **Salmon Quinoa Salad**:
- **Protein**: **Wild-caught salmon** (150g) – top source of **vitamin D**, **omega-3 fatty acids (EPA/DHA)**, and **B12**.
- **Grain**: **Quinoa** (1 cup) – contains all essential amino acids, high in **magnesium** and **iron**.
- **Vegetables**:
  - **Kale** (1 cup) – extremely rich in **vitamin K**, **vitamin A**, and **calcium**.
  - **Bell peppers** (1/2 cup) – provides **vitamin C** and **antioxidants**.
  - **Tomatoes** (1/2 cup) – high in **lycopene**, an antioxidant that supports heart health.
- **Dressing**: **Extra virgin olive oil** (1 tbsp) – rich in **polyphenols** and **monounsaturated fats** for heart health.

### Afternoon Snack:
#### **Greek Yogurt with Berries & Dark Chocolate**:
- **Greek yogurt** (1 cup) – a great source of **calcium**, **probiotics**, and **protein**.
- **Raspberries** (1/2 cup) – full of **vitamin C** and **fiber**.
- **Dark chocolate** (70% cacao, 1 oz) – packed with **iron, magnesium**, and **antioxidants**.

### Dinner:
#### **Grass-fed Beef Stir-Fry**:
- **Protein**: **Grass-fed beef** (150g) – excellent source of **heme iron**, **zinc**, and **B vitamins** (especially **B12**).
- **Vegetables**:
  - **Broccoli** (1 cup) – high in **vitamin C**, **vitamin K**, and **fiber**.
  - **Carrots** (1/2 cup) – provides **beta-carotene** (vitamin A) for skin and eye health.
  - **Shiitake mushrooms** (1/2 cup) – rich in **vitamin D** and **selenium**.
- **Starch**: **Sweet potatoes** (1 cup) – full of **vitamin A**, **fiber**, and **potassium**.
- **Fat**: **Coconut oil** (1 tbsp) – contains medium-chain triglycerides (MCTs), which can be quickly utilized for energy.

### Evening Snack:
#### **Chia Pudding**:
- **Chia seeds** (2 tbsp) – rich in **omega-3 fatty acids**, **fiber**, and **calcium**.
- **Coconut milk** (1/2 cup) – good source of **MCTs** and healthy fats.
- **Toppings**: **Sliced kiwi** (1/2) – provides **vitamin C** and **fiber**.

---

### Summary of Key Nutrients:
- **Vitamin A**: Sweet potatoes, carrots, papaya.
- **Vitamin B12**: Salmon, grass-fed beef, kefir.
- **Vitamin C**: Bell peppers, berries, kiwi.
- **Vitamin D**: Wild-caught salmon, shiitake mushrooms, fortified kefir.
- **Vitamin E**: Almonds, avocado, olive oil.
- **Vitamin K**: Kale, spinach, broccoli.
- **Iron**: Grass-fed beef (heme iron), quinoa, dark chocolate (non-heme iron).
- **Calcium**: Kefir, Greek yogurt, chia seeds.
- **Magnesium**: Almonds, spinach, quinoa.
- **Zinc**: Grass-fed beef, salmon, pumpkin seeds.
- **Omega-3 Fatty Acids**: Salmon, flaxseeds, chia seeds.
- **Selenium**: Brazil nuts, shiitake mushrooms, salmon.

This menu provides an abundance of essential vitamins, minerals, and healthy fats, promoting overall well-being and optimizing nutrient absorption. It's also rich in fiber, antioxidants, and probiotics, ensuring a healthy gut and immune system.
