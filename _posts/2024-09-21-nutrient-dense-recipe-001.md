Nutrient-dense meal plan that incorporates some of the best sources of vitamins, minerals, and other beneficial compounds:

Breakfast:

1. Scrambled eggs (2-3) with spinach and mushrooms
   - Rich in vitamin B12, vitamin D, iron, selenium, and choline
2. Slice of whole grain bread with avocado
   - Provides fiber, vitamin E, and healthy fats
3. Small handful of Brazil nuts (2-3 nuts)
   - Excellent source of selenium
4. Kiwi fruit
   - High in vitamin C

Mid-morning snack:
1. Greek yogurt with mixed berries and chia seeds
   - Provides probiotics, calcium, antioxidants, and omega-3 fatty acids

Lunch:

1. Grilled wild salmon
   - High in omega-3 fatty acids, vitamin D, and selenium
2. Sweet potato
   - Rich in beta-carotene (vitamin A precursor)
3. Steamed broccoli
   - High in vitamin C, vitamin K, and folate
4. Sautéed kale with garlic
   - Excellent source of vitamin K, vitamin C, and antioxidants

Afternoon snack:
1. Carrots and red bell pepper slices with hummus
   - Provides beta-carotene, vitamin C, and healthy fats

Dinner:

1. Grass-fed beef liver (small portion)
   - Extremely rich in vitamin A, B vitamins, iron, and copper
2. Quinoa
   - Complete protein source, rich in minerals
3. Roasted Brussels sprouts
   - High in vitamin K and vitamin C
4. Lentils
   - Good source of iron, folate, and fiber
5. Sauerkraut (as a side)
   - Provides probiotics and vitamin K

Before bed:
1. Small glass of tart cherry juice
   - Contains melatonin, which may aid sleep

Throughout the day:
- Drink plenty of water
- Consider green tea for additional antioxidants

This meal plan provides a wide array of essential nutrients from whole food sources. To optimize absorption:

1. Pair iron-rich foods (like beef liver and lentils) with vitamin C sources (like bell peppers or broccoli) to enhance iron absorption.
2. Consume vitamin D-rich foods (like salmon and eggs) with a source of fat to aid absorption.
3. Lightly cook vegetables to preserve nutrients while making some more bioavailable.
4. Include fermented foods (like yogurt and sauerkraut) to support gut health and nutrient absorption.
