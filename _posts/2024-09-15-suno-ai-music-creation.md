I recently came across [Suno.com](https://suno.com/), a platform that allows you to create AI-generated music so I decided to create a song for my boys.

It’s a pretty straightforward tool where you can input lyrics, choose a genre, and let the AI do the rest to produce a song.

The site doesn't require any musical expertise, making it accessible to anyone who wants to experiment with music creation.

I gave it a try and decided to make a song for my two boys, Harry and Jackson.

The prompt I used was something like :

> A song about two brothers named Harry and Jackon that like to go to the beach and play soccer in the style of a rock and roll song


When you generate a song it'll create two versions of the song for you, the songs are really great and the boys like listening to them :

Suno also gives you the ability to download the songs as MP3 or an MP4 video with an AI generated image thumbnail and the lyrics to the song in the video.

I had high hopes for being able to generate other good songs but haven't really been able to generate any other decent songs.

I've also tried playing around with other AI Music generation sites like <https://www.udio.com/> which is pretty good, seems to be a bit more natural and less AI like but still the two songs above were the best I've come up with.
